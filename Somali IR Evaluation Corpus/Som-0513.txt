Shirkii Jimciyadda Islaamiga Waqooyiga Ameerika IANA oo shalay Phoenix AZ ku soo gabagaboobay.
Axad, November, 20, 2022 (HOL) - Shalay Sabti ahayd  waxaa  magaalada Phoenix ee Gobolka Arizona Masjidka Al Rahma  lagu soo gabgabeeyey shirkii Jimciyadda Islaamiga ee Waqooyiga America, Islamic Association of North Ameica (IANA). Shirkan ayaa si rasmi ah uga socday magaaladda mudo labo cisho ah, Shirkan oo ah shir sannadkiiba mar la qabto ayaa waxaa ka soo qaybgala Masaajidada ay ku midaysan yahiin jimciyadaan oo dhan ilaa 40 xarumood oo kula yaal badi goboladda ay Soomaalida ka dagan yahiin dalka Maraykanka



Waxaana jimciyadaan la aasaasay sandkii 2001, waxayna uga wada hadlayeen sanadkaan qodobbo muhiima oo ugu hareeyaan arimahha dhalinyarada Soomaaliyeed ee dalkaan horemarinta adeegyada dacwada, sidii loo horemarin lahaa mashaariicda masaajida loona wacyigalin lahaa dadka Muslimiinta ah ee ku nool W/ America, waxaa siminaar socday saacado lagu qaatay habka saxda ah ee loo heli karo guryo aan ribo lahayn,iyadoo qabiir arimahaa aqoon sare u leh uu shirka kala soo qaybgalay
