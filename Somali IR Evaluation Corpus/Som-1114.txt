Madaxweyne Putin “Yevgeny Prigozhin waxa uu ahaa nin xumaan iyo wanaagba leh”
Madaxweynaha Ruushka, Vladimir Putin ayaa ka hadlay wararka isa soo taraya ee sheegaya in shil diyaaradeed uu ku dhintay Madaxii xoogaga calooshood u shaqeystayaasha Ruushka ee Wagner, Yevgeny Prigozhin.

Madaxweynaha Ruushka ayaa ka tacsiyeeyay shilka diyaaradeed ee loo maleynayo inay Arbacadii ku dhinteen toban qof oo uu ka mid ahaa Hoggaamiyihii Wagner.

“Marka hore waxaan rabaa inaan tacsi tiiraanyo leh u diro dhammaanba qoysaska dhibaneyaasha. Had iyo jeer waa masiibo shilalka diyaaradaha.” Ayuu yiri Madaxweynaha oo xalay ka soo muuqday Telefishinka Qaranka ee dalkaasi.

Madaxweynaa ayaa tilmaamay in muddo dheer uu garanayay Yevgeny Prigozhin, isla-markaana uu ahaa nin xumaan iyo wanaag, labadaba leh.

“Waxaan garanayay Prigozhin waqti aad u dheer, illaa horraantii 90-meeyadii. Waxa uu ahaa nin isu miriqsan oo wanaag iyo xumaanba leh, wuxuu galay khaladaad halis ku ah noloshiisa, balse hibada uu lahaa ayaa ka dhigtay inuu waxqabad wanaagsan la yimaado.” Ayuu yiri Madaxweynuhu.

Waxa kaloo uu yiri “Waxa uu ahaa qof ama ganacsade karti leh, kamuu shaqayn dalkayaga oo keliya, balse sidoo kale waxa uu ka soo shaqeeyay dibadda, gaar ahaan Afrika. Waxa uu halkaas ka sameeyay ganacsiga saliidda, gaaska, macdanta iyo dhagxaanta qaaliga ah.”
